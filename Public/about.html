Frequently asked questions

On the homepage, click on search and filter, which will provide you with options you can choose from based on your budget. In the search bar, you search for the university you are in, or have been admitted to, then check all filters according to your wishes, and click apply filters. The houses that will then be displayed, will be relevant to that.

After Searching for house listings according to your wishes. You can click on 'See more details' to views more details of the house you like i.e Reviews, Ratings, etc.. If the house pleases you, at the bottom you can click book room, then pay the service fee and automatically the landlord will be notified.

After paying the service fee, the landlord will receive your contacts, and we will also send you the contacts for easier communication afterwards

No, adding a house to your cart does not mean that you have booked it. You use your cart to store the listing that pleases you, so that you can easily find it later. And you can keep on looking at other available listings.
